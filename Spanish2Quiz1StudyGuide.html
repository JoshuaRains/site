9. Study Tips

  • Drill Vocabulary: Make flashcards for colors, days, months, and commands.
  • Write Out Numbers: Practice large and small numbers daily.
  • Role-Play: Greet a friend, share your name, tell the time in Spanish, etc.
  • Listen & Spell: Get a partner to spell words aloud in Spanish; you write them down.
  • Pronunciation: Focus on vowel sounds and rolling the "r" (if you can).
