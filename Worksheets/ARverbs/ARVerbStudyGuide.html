AR Verbs Quiz Study Guide


1. Instructions: Memorize each of the following verbs with their definitions
  • Hablar - to talk/speak
  • Sacar - to take out
  • Usar - to use
  • Caminar - to walk
  • Buscar - to search for
  • Mirar - to look at/watch
  • Tocar - to touch/play (an instrument)
2. Instructions: Memorize the correct AR verb ending for each of the following pronouns.
yo: -o nosotros: -amos
tú: -as
él/ella/usted: -a ellos/ellas/ustedes: -an
3. Instructions: Know the steps to conjugate. Here is an example for the verb "caminar":

We start with the base form: caminar

Step 1: remove the "ar" → camin

Step 2: add the ending that matches the pronoun → camino
