Direct Object Pronouns & Valentine's Day Verbs
Review: Me llamo, Te llamas

Think about how we say Me llamo and Te llamas. The words me and te are pronouns. Today, we will learn how to use them as direct object pronouns!

Direct Object Pronouns

We use direct object pronouns to replace the direct object in a sentence. Here are the pronouns:

  • me - me
  • te - you
  • lo/la - him/her/it
  • nos - us
  • os - you all (Spain)
  • los/las - them

Example: Yo te quiero. (I love you.)
