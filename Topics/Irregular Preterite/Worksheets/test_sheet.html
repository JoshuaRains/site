Note: There are NO ACCENTS for these preterite irregular endings.

In the stem for decir and traer in the Ellos(as)/Uds. form, the "i" is dropped, so they end in -eron instead of -ieron.

CAR, GAR, ZAR

Change occurs only in the YO form.

CAR GAR ZAR
yo yo yo

-AR Preterite Endings: é, aste, ó, amos, aron

Examples:

  • Empezar → empecé
  • Pescar → pesqué
  • Tocar → toqué
  • Pagar → pagué
  • Jugar → jugué
  • Almorzar → almorcé
